### 94205-51 (43MB MFM Hard Drive)

#### Specifications
- 43 megabyte capacity - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Half-height form factor - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Voice coil actuator (not stepper) - very quiet operation - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- 989 cylinders, 5 heads, 17 sectors - Source: [bgW5tpyJljM]
- Manufactured 1988 (late for MFM technology) - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Plastic cover on bottom, PCB on back - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Auto-parking heads - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]

#### Performance
- ~289 KB/sec transfer with non-optimized interleave - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- ~522 KB/sec maximum data rate with optimal interleave - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- 26.3ms average seek time - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- 3.9ms track-to-track seek - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- RPM: approximately 3600 (slightly off is normal) - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Optimal interleave: 1:1 on fast 486 DX2/66 - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]

#### Voice Coil Benefits
- Very quiet seeking compared to stepper drives - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Only audible noise is disc spinning - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
- Quick random seeks sound fast and smooth - Source: [CpQ2j2J6wL0]
