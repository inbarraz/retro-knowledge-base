## Platform-Specific Knowledge

### LS-120 SuperDisk Drive

#### Overview
- High-capacity floppy drive technology by Imation (3M spin-off) - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- 120MB capacity on special LS-120 disks - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Backwards compatible with standard 1.44MB and 720KB floppies - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Uses laser servo tracking for higher track density - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- IDE/ATAPI interface (connects like CD-ROM) - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]

#### Reliability Issues
- Prone to mechanical failures - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Head alignment issues common over time - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Many units found non-functional today - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- LS-120 disks themselves more reliable than drives - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]

#### Compatibility
- Works as standard floppy replacement for 1.44MB disks - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Some BIOS versions support booting from LS-120 - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Can be used for retrocomputing disk imaging - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Useful for transferring files to legacy systems - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]

#### Parts Availability
- Tandon-made mechanisms common in LS-120 drives - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Some parts interchangeable with standard floppy drives - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
- Spindle motors and head assemblies sometimes compatible - Source: [GUvasXbxUPg]
