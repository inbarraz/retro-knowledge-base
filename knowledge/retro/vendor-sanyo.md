## Platform-Specific Knowledge

### Sanyo TPM 2570 / Sears "Binocular TV"

#### Overview
- Tiny portable TV with unique binocular-shaped form factor - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Sears model 564 (rebadged Sanyo TPM 2570) - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Made in Japan for Sears and Roebuck - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Manufactured 1980 - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- CRT approximately 54mm (just over 2 inches) diameter - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]

#### Features
- TV FM AM bands - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- VHF band selector (two VHF bands for North America) plus UHF - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Volume slider and headphone jack - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Telescopic antenna - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Geared tuning dial for fine tuning - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Tilting bail stand with living hinge (may become brittle over time) - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]

#### Power
- 9V DC input, center negative - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- 2.5 watts power consumption - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Battery compartment for 6x AA cells - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Also accepts rechargeable battery pack - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Switch selects between dry cell and rechargeable pack operation - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- DC input disconnects battery when plugged in - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]

#### Controls
- Off/Charge/On power switch - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Vertical hold, contrast, brightness adjustments on back - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
- Charge LED on front - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]

#### Condition Notes

- Some linearity issues (squished at top vs bottom) typical - Source: [S6H8pOKZCPQ]
